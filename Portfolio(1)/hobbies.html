MUSIC

John Frusciante is the guitarist and backing vocalist of the band Red Hot Chili Peppers. The band, which started music in the early 90s, generally makes music in the style of alternative rock and funk rock. The group, which was on the rise until 2006, has many beautiful albums. However, apart from the group, John Frusciante continued to show his artistic intelligence and talents by making solo albums. In addition to the general musical style in the group, he has adopted a different style in each of his solo albums, which shows his artistic acumen. After about 15 years of separation, he decides to join the group again in 2021 and the group then releases 2 more beautiful albums. Below you can find some songs from John's solo albums.
