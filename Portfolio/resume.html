More About Myself

Afterwards, I took 'Introduction to object-oriented programming with Java' and 'Object-oriented and functional programming with Java' trainings to fully understand the logic of object-oriented programming. I supported these trainings with mobile application development training using 'Java & Kotlin' languages. In addition to the competencies I have mentioned so far, I would like to contribute to the organization in the best way and add value to the organization with all my efforts by blending my desire to learn new things, my determination to work and my high motivation, analytical thinking and problem-solving skills, as well as my teamwork skills and communication skills.
